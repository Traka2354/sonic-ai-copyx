Frequently Asked Questions

Is copy trading legal?

Yes. Copy trading is a fully legal, regulated financial service. Tag Markets is a licensed broker, and copy trading is an accepted form of social investing used by millions of retail investors worldwide. All trades are executed on a regulated platform and are fully transparent.

What is the difference between the 12X and 24X accounts?

Both accounts copy the same Sonic AI gold trading strategy. The difference is the leverage multiplier applied to each copied trade. The 12X account uses 12x leverage (lower risk, lower potential return), while the 24X account uses 24x leverage (higher potential return, but also higher risk). Always choose the account that matches your risk tolerance.

Do I need any trading experience?

No experience is required. Sonic AI CopyX handles everything automatically — trade selection, entry, exit, and risk management. You simply choose your account type, deposit funds, activate copy trading, and monitor your results.

What is the minimum deposit?

Please check the current Tag Markets deposit requirements during registration. You can also open a free Demo account first to explore the platform with virtual funds before committing any real money.

How and when are profits distributed?

Profits are calculated and made available weekly. You can withdraw to your preferred payment method at any time — there are no lock-up periods. Withdrawal processing times depend on your chosen method and Tag Markets' standard procedures.

Can I stop or change my copy trading settings at any time?

Yes, absolutely. You are always in full control of your account. You can stop copying, change your lot size, adjust your stop-loss, or withdraw your funds at any time through the Tag Markets platform. There are no penalties for stopping.

Are my funds safe with Tag Markets?

Tag Markets is a regulated broker. Client funds are held in segregated accounts, separate from the company's operational funds. This is a standard regulatory requirement that protects your deposit. However, all trading involves market risk — your capital is at risk, and losses are possible. Please read the full risk disclosure.
